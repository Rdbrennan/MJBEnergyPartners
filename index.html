About Us

MJB Energy Resources, Inc., through Michael J. Brennan brings a team of 30+ years experience in construction, engineering,
banking & tax expertise that will define, implement & follow-up service energy enhancements or renewable new technology strategies.

We custom design the clients “energy savings strategies” prudently tailored to fit a corporations sustainability & environmental challenges in giving back to the its respective communities.

Our Commitment

To provide our clients with the most prudent of products & services available in clean energy that achieves results for employee production, safest work-space availability, and the most energy efficient savings which results in generating a solid “Return on Investment.”

Services We Offer

Utility Rebates

 

We help clients understand their utility rebates based upon kilowatt usage, capital investment, employee growth & average wage for up to a 10 year contract.

Lighting Strategy

 

We design a long-term lighting & maintenance strategy based upon the lighting needs for the work-space below the fixture, i.e.; office, production, warehouse, storage, racking system, etc.

Capital Stack

 

Tailoring the energy management service in providing the least cost associated with implementing the project whether cash or debt.

Tax Benefits

 

Whether you own a warehouse, corporate office, or a recreational facility, all businesses need to minimize their tax liability and risk. We Seek out the highest tax efficient strategy by either the CARE Act or EPAct applicable to the 179D schedule.
